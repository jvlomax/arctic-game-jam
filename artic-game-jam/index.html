Calling all developers, come join us at the inaugural Arctic Game Jam event in Tromsø on the 12th - 14th of September! Jointly organized by Tromsø’s own Bedriftsnettverk for spill & interaktiv produksjoner - this is an awesome opportunity for professionals and enthusiasts alike to network and take on an active role in the fast-growing development scene in Northern Norway. The Arctic Game Jam 2014 will be held at Flow: Co-Working and Innovation Space. The aim is for participants to work on developing a game idea based around a theme over a 48-hour period. We’re expecting nothing less than a marathon session of devious designs and courageous coding! Ready for the challenge? Registration is now open at www.arcticgamejam.com with limited space available, so hurry and sign up now! Who can join? The Arctic Game Jam is open to anyone with an interest in developing games/interactive concepts meaning that participants with programming skills are particularly relevant. But making good games often take a variety of skillsets so we welcome artists, musicians and writers. You can always team up with programmers you meet at the event, find helpful tools that can help you flesh out your ideas, or consult with the various professional game developers at the event!
What else is happening at Arctic Game Jam 2014?
Seeing as how it’s the first time we’re getting together, we’ve prepared some extra side activities to commemorate the occasion -
Friday 12th September 7pm - 8pm: Pizza time and theme reveal at Arctic Game Jam 2014
Friday, 12th September 8pm -9pm : Game Development Boot Camp by Kim Daniel Arthur
Saturday, 13th September 1pm - 3pm: Open Consultation/Troubleshooting - Participants can submit questions and seek consultation with professional members of Tromsø’s Bedriftsnettverk for Spill&Interaktiv to ramp up their demo development.
Saturday 13th September 6pm - 7pm: Pizza Time II - Free food for all registered participants
Sunday 14th September 12pm -2pm: Commit! - All participants are meant to submit their game demo which will then be featured in respective order
Sunday 14th September 3pm: Results of Arctic Game Jam - Demos will be appraised by our select panel and the winning entry for the Arctic Game Jam 2014 will be judged
Rules:
  • Participants must be at least 15 years or older to take part
  • Participants can develop solo or in teams with a maximum of 4 person per team
  • Participants are expected to develop an original game idea within 48hrs that must be based around a theme that will be revealed at the start of the Arctic Game Jam.
  • Solo participants, or at least a representative member of their team should be in attendance on location by 8pm on Friday, 12th of September
  • You are expected to bring your own computer equipment and power cords and/or extensions.
  • Tables and seating arrangements at the game jam venue are on a first come, first served basis.
  • You’re allowed to sleep at the venue - so it’s advisable to bring sleeping bags if you’re aiming to get some shuteye at some point of the Arctic Game Jam!
  • You’re also allowed to bring your own food and beverages, although copious amount of pizza and snacks will be provided.
  • Participants are expected to clean up after themselves and if intoxicated, will risk being expelled from the venue.
  • Participants are responsible for the safety of their own equipment.
