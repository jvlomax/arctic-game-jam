Arctic Game Jam 2014

Calling all game enthusiasts and creative people, come join us at the inaugural Arctic Game Jam event in Tromsø on the 12th - 14th of September!

Jointly organized by Tromsø’s own Bedriftsnettverk for spill & interaktiv produksjoner and Tromsøstudentenes dataforening - this is an awesome opportunity for professionals and enthusiasts alike to network and take on an active role in the fast-growing development scene in Northern Norway.


The Arctic Game Jam 2014 will be held at Flow: Co-Working and Innovation Space. The aim is for participants to work on developing a game idea based around a theme over a 48-hour period. We’re expecting nothing less than a marathon session of devious designs and courageous coding!



Who can join?

The Arctic Game Jam is open to anyone with an interest in developing games/interactive concepts meaning that participants with programming skills are particularly relevant.

Making good games often take a variety of skillsets so we welcome artists, musicians and writers. You can always team up with programmers you meet at the event, find helpful tools that can help you flesh out your ideas, or consult with the various professional game developers at the event!

What else is happening at Arctic Game Jam 2014?

  • Seeing as how it’s the first time we’re getting together, we’ve prepared some extra side activities to commemorate the occasion -

  • Friday 12th September 7pm - 8pm: Pizza time and theme reveal at Arctic Game Jam 2014

  • Friday, 12th September 8pm -9pm : Game Development Boot Camp by Kim Daniel Arthur

  • Saturday, 13th September 1pm - 3pm: Open Consultation/Troubleshooting - Participants can submit questions and seek consultation with professional members of Tromsø’s Bedriftsnettverk for Spill&Interaktiv Produksjoner to ramp up their demo development.

  • Saturday 13th September 6pm - 7pm: Pizza Time II - Free food for all registered participants

  • Sunday 14th September 12pm -2pm: Commit! - All participants are meant to submit their game demo which will then be featured in respective order

  • Sunday 14th September 3pm: Results of Arctic Game Jam - Demos will be appraised by our select panel and the winning entry for the Arctic Game Jam 2014 will be judged and prizes will be awarded.
