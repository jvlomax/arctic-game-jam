What else is happening at Arctic Game Jam 2014?

  • Seeing as how it’s the first time we’re getting together, we’ve prepared some extra side activities to commemorate the occasion -

  • Friday 12th September 7pm - 8pm: Pizza time and theme reveal at Arctic Game Jam 2014

  • Friday, 12th September 8pm -9pm : Game Development Boot Camp by Kim Daniel Arthur

  • Saturday, 13th September 1pm - 3pm: Open Consultation/Troubleshooting - Participants can submit questions and seek consultation with professional members of Tromsø’s Bedriftsnettverk for Spill&Interaktiv Produksjoner to ramp up their demo development.

  • Saturday 13th September 6pm - 7pm: Pizza Time II - Free food for all registered participants

  • Sunday 14th September 12pm -2pm: Commit! - All participants are meant to submit their game demo which will then be featured in respective order

  • Sunday 14th September 3pm: Results of Arctic Game Jam - Demos will be appraised by our select panel and the winning entry for the Arctic Game Jam 2014 will be judged and prizes will be awarded.
