Rules:
  • Participants must be at least 15 years or older to take part
  • Participants can develop solo or in teams with a maximum of 4 person per team
  • Participants are expected to develop an original game idea within 48hrs that must be based around a theme that will be revealed at the start of the Arctic Game Jam.
  • Solo participants, or at least a representative member of their team should be in attendance on location by 8pm on Friday, 12th of September
  • You are expected to bring your own computer equipment and power cords and/or extensions.
  • Tables and seating arrangements at the game jam venue are on a first come, first served basis.
  • You’re allowed to sleep at the venue - so it’s advisable to bring sleeping bags if you’re aiming to get some shuteye at some point of the Arctic Game Jam!
  • You’re also allowed to bring your own food and beverages, although copious amount of pizza and snacks will be provided.
  • Participants are expected to clean up after themselves and if intoxicated, will risk being expelled from the venue.
  • Participants are responsible for the safety of their own equipment.
